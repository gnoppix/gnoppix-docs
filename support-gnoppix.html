Chapter 16.

Support Gnoppix

Donate to fight surveillance and censorship
In this chapter:
  • List of Gnoppix Lovers and Supporters

Gnoppix is and will remain Free Software because the most vulnerable and oppressed people are the most in need of privacy and security.

  • Journalists and whistleblowers use Gnoppix to expose government and corporate wrongdoing.
  • Activists use Gnoppix to avoid surveillance and organize their struggles for liberatory social change.
  • Domestic violence survivors use Gnoppix to escape surveillance at home.
  • Privacy-concerned citizens use Gnoppix to avoid online tracking and censorship.

Gnoppix is their strongest protection

Donations from passionate people like you are our most valuable source of funding because they guarantee our independence. As you know, open source projects like ours are maintained entirely by volunteers and do not generate any income. We rely on donations to cover our operational costs and to keep the project running smoothly.

Your generous donations are essential for keeping this project alive and ensuring that our users continue to benefit from the advancements we make. Every donation, no matter how small, helps us to continue our work.
